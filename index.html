Summary: Gun Violence Project

Gun violence is known to affect lives in America. This project aimed to determine which states of the US experience frequent mass shootings over a period of time, and the impact it has on the overall death toll. The death toll of teens and children separately were also examined.

In this project, ETL was performed for gun violence data including mass shooting and casualties numbers in children and teens since 2014.
